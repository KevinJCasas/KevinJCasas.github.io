Cardinal Booster Club

IWA Cardinal Boosters Club Mission

The Cardinal Boosters Club is a recognized parent organization with a focus on charitable efforts on campus.

  • Our purpose it is to encourage enthusiastic support and interest in Incarnate Word Academy and its students.
  • Support ALL Incarnate Word Academy Students!
  • Contribute to the advancement and needs of educational programs at Incarnate Word Academy.
  • Lend support to the faculty, staff, and administration.

We will achieve these goals by organizing events, fundraisers and assist in building the Pride and Spirit at Incarnate Word Academy!

Mission Statement

The IWA Cardinal Boosters Club is a charitable, parent organization whose purpose is to encourage enthusiastic support and interest in Incarnate Word Academy Students, to contribute to the advancement and needs of educational programs at IWA; to lend support to the faculty, staff, and administration.

Statement of Purpose

The IWA Cardinal Boosters Club is being organized to provide support to the students and campus of the Incarnate Word Academy. We intend to provide financial support for educational programs, contribute to the spirit of Incarnate Word Academy and lend support as needed to faculty, staff, and administration.
