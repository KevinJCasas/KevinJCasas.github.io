Chapter Critiques #1

Caption

These are the critiques that I received from my classmates for the first version of the second chapter for my novel. There are some kind words about my writing and a lot of great questions and feedback that the class provided for my consideration during the revision process of the chapter.

This was the first time that I really felt like I had been given constructive feedback from my peers for a written project. They commented on my choices in dialogue, told me what they would like to see more of in the chapter, and asked questions about the world I had created. I found it all to be incredibly motivating and made me want to get back to writing, a first experience for me. I learned that I thrive off of clear feedback that helps me make better decisions moving forward.
