Novel Chapters

Caption

These are the two submissions of my second chapter for the Fiction Workshop that I took this semester. The first version of the chapter came from the ideas that I had talked through with my friend and that I continued to deliberate on until I knew the deadline for the chapter was approaching. It took me four days of intermittent writing to complete the chapter, and I felt good about what I had written. There were certainly things that needed to be changed, and with the feedback from my classmates I revised the chapter, splitting it into two chapters and focusing on making changes to the first part.

These artifacts show how much the feedback from my peers helped me improve my writing. I appreciated having the ability to write what was a draft of the chapter and get feedback from the critiques and in-class discussion about my ideas. Those comments helped me to choose what to keep and what to take out in the revised version.
