Fiction Workshop Notes

Caption

These are just some of the notes that I wrote down when trying to decide what I wanted to happen in the second chapter of my novel. While I was taking these notes, I was also talking to a friend that is also a writer. I had some major questions for them regarding the plot and story arc of the characters, but it soon became clear that I needed to spend more time thinking about the world and themes I wanted in the story. As you can see, there are a lot of ideas and questions that I was trying to sort through even after talking to my friend, and I had a difficult time finally deciding on a direction.

I feel like this artifact is a great indication of how my mind works at processing thoughts. A lot of the notes are half-complete and have big gaps between them where I knew I needed to add more information later. I had to focus on getting the ideas in my head written down before trying to work on filling in the missing parts. Talking about the ideas out loud helped me to pinpoint certain ideas and clarify them further so that I had a more solid understanding of what I needed to write.
